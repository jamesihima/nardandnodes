The Name

Nard & Notes.

Nard

Spikenard — the precious aromatic oil, historically among the most costly perfumes in the world; associated with anointing, devotion, and ceremony.

Notes

A triple meaning — the fragrance notes of top, heart, and base; the musical notes of composition and harmony; and the written note, a letter, a kept memory.

Together — fragrance as something precious, composed, and personal. A wearable memory.

Mission

To transform ordinary events into unforgettable sensory experiences by empowering every guest to create their own signature scent — turning fragrance into a personal, wearable memory.

Vision

To become the leading name in experiential luxury fragrance across the U.S. and beyond — redefining how people connect with scent by making bespoke perfumery accessible, interactive, and deeply personal at life's most meaningful moments.
