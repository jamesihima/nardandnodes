Questions & Notes
How much space does the bar need?+

The mobile bar fits gracefully into most venues — a draped footprint of roughly six to eight feet is ample. We adapt to your floor plan and styling.

How long does each guest take?+

Most guests blend in eight to twelve minutes. Across a two-to-three hour experience, the bar comfortably serves a flowing crowd.

Do you travel to our venue?+

Yes. The bar is fully mobile and travels to your venue. Destination and private celebrations are warmly welcome.

How many guests can you serve?+

Experiences are tailored to your guest count and run two to three hours. Share your numbers and we'll recommend the right setup.

How far in advance should we book?+

We recommend reserving four to six weeks ahead. Bespoke and private-label work benefits from a little more lead time.
